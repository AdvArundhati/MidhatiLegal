# Midhati Legal Aid Foundation

## About Us

Midhati Legal Aid Foundation is dedicated to promoting legal awareness, access to justice, and social empowerment through education, guidance, and legal support.

Our mission is to help citizens understand their rights and responsibilities while providing assistance to vulnerable sections of society, including senior citizens, victims of domestic violence, women, children, and animals.

## Vision

To build a legally aware and empowered society where every individual has equal access to justice, legal knowledge, and professional support without fear, confusion, or financial barriers.

## Services

* Free legal consultation
* Legal awareness programs
* Rights education
* Support for senior citizens
* Assistance for domestic violence victims
* Legal research and documentation
* Social justice initiatives
* Animal welfare advocacy
